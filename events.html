FAQ

Common
Questions

Here are most common
asked questions that we
get

What types of decorations do you offer?

We offer a wide range of decorations including floral arrangements, balloon decorations, table centerpieces, and themed decorations.

Can I customize the decorations according to my event theme?

Yes, we provide customization options to match your event theme. Our team will work closely with you to create personalized decorations that suit your preferences.

Do you provide delivery and setup services for the decorations?

Yes, we offer delivery and setup services for all our decorations. Our team will ensure that the decorations are delivered and set up at your event venue according to your specified requirements.

How far in advance should I place an Book for decorations?

We recommend placing an Book for decorations at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability. However, we understand that some events may require last-minute arrangements, so please contact us to check for availability.

What is your refund policy for decorations?

We have a flexible refund policy for decorations. If you need to cancel your Book, please notify us at least 48 hours before the scheduled delivery or setup time to receive a full refund. For cancellations made within 48 hours, a partial refund may be provided.
